This article explores the early onset of computer viruses, the different types of viruses possible, as well as some of the historical ramifications of specific viruses that affected individuals/groups in the past. Computer viruses can share similarities with biological viruses, and can share some of the same characteristics such as infectivity, invisibility, destructibility, and unpredictability.
